About Me

Diving Headfirst Into Tech

Web development is the perfect mix of problem-solving with creative expression. Taking on the challenge of coding websites from scratch and getting them to work exactly as intended is a puzzle that fascinates me. The fact that there’s always something new to learn has converted me into a lifelong student of Internet technology.

Cultivating the seed of an idea, watching it grow into something real, and then sharing it with the entire world is profoundly exciting. To that end, I look forward to developing your websites and transforming your own visions into reality. Let's build something awesome!

What I'm Doing Now

Currently I'm exploring object-oriented programming in more depth. Experimenting with responsive elements to make applications more integrative has opened up a realm of new possibilities, so check back for more surprises later.

I spend a lot of time thinking about sustainable ecology in the context of systems analysis. How I can use my tech skills to make the planet more habitable in responsible, scalable ways? That is the ultimate question!

When I step away from my keyboard, you’ll find me weightlifting while I jam to my favorite rock tunes or hiking in the surrounding mountains (where, I assume, I am always being stalked by cougars and tabbies alike).
